# Account Recovery Policy
Learn how to recover access to your CloudSync Pro account if you are locked out or forgot your credentials.

## Locked Accounts
Accounts are locked automatically after 5 consecutive failed login attempts.
* **Lockout Period**: 30 minutes.
* **Auto-Unlock**: System unlocks the account automatically after the lockout period expires.

## Resetting Password
1. Navigate to the login page and click "Forgot Password?".
2. Enter your registered email address.
3. Check your email for a verification code/link. The link is valid for 15 minutes.
4. If you do not receive the email, check your SPAM folder or contact your organization's administrator to reset it via the Admin Console.

## Verification Questions
If email access is lost, contact Support with:
* Billing invoice number from last 3 months.
* Registered credit card details (last 4 digits only).
* Account creation date or registered domains.
