CloudSync Pro Billing Policy & Account Management

1. Subscriptions and Tiers
CloudSync Pro offers three main subscription tiers:
- Developer Plan: Up to 5 GB storage, basic synchronization.
- Pro Plan: Up to 500 GB storage, real-time replication, 99.9% SLA.
- Enterprise Plan: Custom storage, dedicated clusters, multi-region replication.

2. Payment Tier Changes
Customers can upgrade or downgrade their tiers at any time via the Admin Console under Settings > Billing > Plan. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle, while upgrades apply immediately and are prorated.

3. Corporate Card Processing
We support Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. In case of payment failures, the system will retry processing the corporate card 3 times over 7 days. If payment fails on the 4th attempt, the account will be placed in a "grace period" (restricted read-only access) for 14 days before termination.

4. Direct Account Cancellation Steps
To cancel your CloudSync Pro account:
1. Log into the console as the Account Owner.
2. Navigate to Admin Console > Billing > Close Account.
3. Click "Cancel Subscription".
4. Confirm by typing your organization name.
5. All replicas will be scheduled for deletion 30 days after cancellation.
*Note*: Refunds are not automatically issued, but you can file a request with billing within 48 hours of subscription renewal.
